Citizenship through Naturalization

This is the most common way for foreign-born persons to become a U.S. citizen. Applicants must be 18 years old and fall into one of the three categories below:

  • 5 years as a permanent resident.
  • 3 years as a permanent resident who is married with a U.S. citizen spouse for at least 3 years.
  • Qualifying service in the U.S. armed forces.

    • Here's a link with more information from U.S. Immigration: https://www.uscis.gov/sites/default/files/files/article/chapter4.pdf

    You should file the following: Form N-400, Application for Naturalization. {% endblock %}
